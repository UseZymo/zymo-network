# Zymo

> One AI. Every Device. Infinite Possibilities.

Zymo is a personal AI operating system designed to help people transform ideas into reality. Built around persistent memory, intelligent automation, and seamless cross-device experiences, Zymo acts as your always-available AI companion that remembers context, builds custom tools, controls connected devices, and adapts to your daily workflow.

Instead of switching between dozens of apps, assistants, and interfaces, Zymo provides a unified AI layer capable of understanding your goals and executing tasks wherever you are.

---

# 🚀 Overview

Ideas should become reality instantly.

Zymo empowers users with a personal AI assistant that doesn't just answer questions—it remembers, creates, automates, and acts.

Whether you're building software, managing projects, organizing your life, or controlling smart devices, Zymo becomes the intelligent operating layer connecting everything together.
